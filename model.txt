Database Schema
The database schema includes the following tables:

customers: Stores customer information.
products: Stores product information.
orders: Stores information about customer orders.
order_items: Stores details about each item in an order.
reviews: Stores customer reviews of products.


Explanation
customers: Stores details about each customer.
products: Stores products available for sale.
orders: Stores information about customer purchases.
order_items: Links products to specific orders with quantities and prices.
reviews: Allows customers to leave reviews for products.
